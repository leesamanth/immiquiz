About ImmiQuiz

ImmiQuiz is dedicated to helping immigrants and aspiring US citizens prepare for the naturalization process. Our platform offers a comprehensive set of practice questions, flashcards, and quizzes based on the official US citizenship test.

We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality, free resources to study and succeed. Our quizzes and flashcards are designed to make learning easy, interactive, and effective, whether you're studying alone or with friends and family.

Features:

  • Practice with hundreds of real citizenship test questions
  • Review answers and explanations for every question
  • Use flashcards for quick study sessions
  • Track your progress and improve your score
